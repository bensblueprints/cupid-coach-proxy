cupid.coach — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-19

What stays on your device

Your conversations with your coach, your onboarding profile (what you're looking for and your personal deal-breakers), your tracked contacts and dates, post-date notes, live-date transcripts, and red-flag findings are all stored only on your device (local SQLite storage). We do not upload, sync, or have any access to this content on our servers. If you delete the app, this data is gone.

What we do collect

If you choose to create an account (optional — the app works without one), we store only your email address and an authentication credential via our self-hosted Supabase Auth service, used solely to identify you and gate access to our AI relay service (below) against abuse. We do not use this account to store, back up, or sync any of your dating content.

AI processing (chat & voice transcription)

Text you send your coach and audio you record for transcription are processed using Groq's AI API. On mobile, these requests pass through a relay server we operate that forwards them to Groq using our own API key and your account's sign-in token (to prevent abuse) — this relay does not log, store, or retain any request or response content; it is a stateless pass-through. On desktop, requests go directly from the app to Groq using your own configured API key.

Date Mode — live audio recording

Date Mode records audio during a date to transcribe it and offer real-time coaching suggestions. Recording another person's conversation without their knowledge or consent may be illegal in your jurisdiction (many US states require all-party consent to record a private conversation). This is the user's responsibility to manage — the app includes a settings toggle and a one-time notice about this, but does not enforce disclosure to the other party.

Audio recorded during Date Mode is transcribed via the AI processing described above and the resulting text is stored locally on your device, same as any other date note. It is never uploaded to persistent storage on our servers.

Notifications

Date Mode coaching suggestions are delivered as local, on-device notifications. No push notification service or third-party notification provider is used.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. To delete your account (email/auth record only — this holds no dating content), contact us at the address below.
